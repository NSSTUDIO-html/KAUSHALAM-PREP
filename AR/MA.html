Mixture & Alligation

Concepts • Formulas • Shortcuts • Practice

Basic Concept

Mixture = Combining two or more quantities with different values

Important Formulas

✔ Mean Price = Total Cost / Total Quantity

✔ Replacement Formula = (Quantity removed / Total quantity)

Alligation Rule

✔ Ratio = (Difference of opposite values)

Quantity ratio = (High - Mean) : (Mean - Low)

Shortcut Tricks

✔ Use alligation for fast solving

✔ For replacement → use percentage reduction

✔ Always check units (liters, kg, etc.)

Example

Milk = ₹20, Water = ₹10, Mean = ₹15

Ratio = (20 - 15) : (15 - 10)

= 5 : 5 = 1 : 1

Percentage

Concepts • Formulas • Shortcuts • Practice

Basic Concept

Percentage (%) means "per 100"

x% = x / 100

Important Formulas

✔ Percentage = (Value / Total) × 100

✔ Value = (Percentage × Total) / 100

✔ Increase % = (Increase / Original) × 100

✔ Decrease % = (Decrease / Original) × 100

Important Conversions

1/2 = 50%

1/3 = 33.33%

1/4 = 25%

1/5 = 20%

1/10 = 10%

3/4 = 75%

Shortcut Tricks

✔ x% of y = y% of x

Example: 20% of 50 = 50% of 20 = 10

✔ Increase then decrease same % → Net loss

✔ 10% = divide by 10

✔ 1% = divide by 100

Applications

✔ Profit & Loss

✔ Simple Interest

✔ Data Interpretation

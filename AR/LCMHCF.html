LCM & HCF

Aptitude Concepts • Shortcuts • Practice

HCF (Highest Common Factor)

The largest number that divides all given numbers exactly.

Example: HCF(12,18) = 6

LCM (Least Common Multiple)

The smallest number divisible by all given numbers.

Example: LCM(12,18) = 36

Important Formula

HCF × LCM = Product of Two Numbers

Methods

Prime Factorization

Find prime factors of numbers.

HCF → Lowest powers
LCM → Highest powers

Division Method

Divide numbers by common primes repeatedly.

Shortcut Tricks

✔ LCM = (a × b) / HCF

✔ If HCF = 1 → LCM = Product (Co-prime numbers)

✔ HCF always ≤ smallest number

✔ LCM always ≥ largest number

Examples

24 = 2³ × 3

36 = 2² × 3²

HCF = 2² × 3 = 12

LCM = 2³ × 3² = 72

Practice Questions

1. Find HCF of 16, 24, 32

2. Find LCM of 6, 8, 12

3. If HCF = 5 and product = 200, find LCM

4. Find HCF of 45 and 75

Simple Interest

Concepts • Formulas • Shortcuts • Practice

Principal (P)

Original amount of money

Rate (R)

Interest rate per year (%)

Time (T)

Duration (in years)

Simple Interest (SI)

Extra money earned/paid

Important Formula

SI = (P × R × T) / 100

Amount

Amount = Principal + SI

Shortcut Tricks

✔ SI ∝ P, R, T (directly proportional)

✔ For 1 year → SI = (P × R) / 100

✔ For same P & R → SI ratio = Time ratio

Example: Time 2:3 → SI = 2:3

Examples

P = 1000, R = 10%, T = 2 years

SI = (1000 × 10 × 2) / 100 = 200

Amount = 1200

Practice Questions

1. Find SI if P = 2000, R = 5%, T = 3

2. Find amount for P = 1000, R = 10%, T = 1

3. If SI = 300, P = 1500, T = 2 → find R

4. SI ratio for time 2:5 → find ratio

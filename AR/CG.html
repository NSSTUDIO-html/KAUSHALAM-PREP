Coordinate Geometry

Distance • Midpoint • Lines • Practice

Distance Formula

Distance = √[(x₂ - x₁)² + (y₂ - y₁)²]

Midpoint Formula

Midpoint = ((x₁ + x₂)/2 , (y₁ + y₂)/2)

Slope

Slope (m) = (y₂ - y₁) / (x₂ - x₁)

Equation of Line

y = mx + c

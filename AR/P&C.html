Pipes & Cisterns

Concepts • Formulas • Shortcuts • Practice

Basic Concept

Filling Pipe → Positive Work
Emptying Pipe → Negative Work

Important Formulas

✔ Work = Time × Rate

✔ Rate = 1 / Time

✔ Total work = 1 (tank full)

Types of Pipes

Inlet Pipe

Fills the tank → positive rate

Outlet Pipe

Empties the tank → negative rate

Shortcut Tricks

✔ If pipe fills in x hours → 1 hour work = 1/x

✔ Combined work → add/subtract rates

Example: 1/10 - 1/15

✔ LCM method → fastest solving

Example

Pipe A fills in 10 hrs → rate = 1/10

Pipe B empties in 15 hrs → rate = -1/15

Net work = 1/10 - 1/15 = 1/30

Tank filled in 30 hrs

Practice Questions

1. A pipe fills tank in 12 hrs → find 1 hr work

2. A (10 hrs), B (20 hrs) → find combined time

3. One pipe fills, one empties → find net time

4. Tank filled in parts → find total time

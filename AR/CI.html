Compound Interest

Concepts • Formulas • Shortcuts • Practice

Principal (P)

Original amount

Rate (R)

Interest rate (%)

Time (T)

Number of years

Amount (A)

Final value after interest

Important Formula

A = P × (1 + R/100)T

Compound Interest

CI = Amount - Principal

Special Cases

✔ Half yearly → R/2, T×2

✔ Quarterly → R/4, T×4

Shortcut Tricks

✔ For 2 years:

CI = P × (R/100)²

✔ Difference between CI & SI (2 years):

= P × (R/100)²

✔ Multiply factors instead of full formula

Example

P = 1000, R = 10%, T = 2

A = 1000 × (1.1)² = 1210

CI = 210

Practice Questions

1. Find CI for P = 2000, R = 10%, T = 2

2. Find amount for P = 1500, R = 5%, T = 3

3. Find difference between CI and SI

4. Calculate CI for half yearly compounding

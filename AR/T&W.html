Time & Work

Concepts • Formulas • Shortcuts • Practice

Basic Concept

Work = Time × Efficiency

Important Formulas

✔ Work = Time × Efficiency

✔ Efficiency = Work / Time

✔ Time = Work / Efficiency

Types of Questions

Single Person Work

One person completes work alone

Combined Work

Multiple people working together

Shortcut Tricks

✔ If A can do work in x days → 1 day work = 1/x

✔ Combined work → add efficiencies

Example: A (1/10) + B (1/15)

✔ Efficiency ∝ 1 / Time

Examples

A can complete work in 10 days

B can complete work in 15 days

Combined work per day = 1/10 + 1/15 = 1/6

Total time = 6 days

Practice Questions

1. A can do work in 12 days → find 1 day work

2. A (10 days), B (20 days) → find combined time

3. If A is twice efficient as B → find ratio

4. Work completed in 5 days by 5 people → find efficiency

📖 Verbal Reasoning - Complete Concepts

Analogy

Find relationship between two pairs.

Example: Dog : Bark :: Cat : Meow

Classification

Group similar items and find the different one.

Example: Apple, Mango, Car, Banana → Car is odd

Odd One Out

Identify the element that does not belong to the group.

Statement & Conclusion

Check whether conclusion logically follows from statement.

Tip: Avoid assumptions beyond given data.

Statement & Assumption

Find hidden assumptions behind a statement.

Statement & Argument

Evaluate strength of arguments (strong/weak).

Cause & Effect

Identify relationship between cause and result.

Example: Rain → Flood

Assertion & Reason

Check if reason correctly explains the assertion.

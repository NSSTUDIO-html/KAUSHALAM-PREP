Partnership

Concepts • Formulas • Shortcuts • Practice

Basic Concept

Profit ∝ Investment × Time

Important Formula

Profit Ratio = (Capital × Time)

Types of Partnership

Simple Partnership

All partners invest for same time

Compound Partnership

Different investment times

Shortcut Tricks

✔ Multiply capital with time → find ratio

✔ Divide profit in that ratio

✔ Use LCM for faster solving

Example

A invests ₹1000 for 1 year

B invests ₹2000 for 1 year

Ratio = 1000 : 2000 = 1 : 2

Profit divided in 1:2

Practice Questions

1. A invests 1000, B invests 1500 → find ratio

2. A invests for 1 year, B for 6 months → find ratio

3. Profit = 3000 → divide in ratio

4. Find share of each partner

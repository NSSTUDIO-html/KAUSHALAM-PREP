1. Parts of Speech

Noun, Pronoun, Verb, Adjective, Adverb, Preposition, Conjunction, Interjection.

  • Noun - Name of person, place, thing
  • Verb - Action word (run, eat, write)
  • Adjective - Describes noun
  • Adverb - Describes verb

2. Tenses

Present, Past, Future and their 12 forms.

  • Present Simple: I eat
  • Past Simple: I ate
  • Future Simple: I will eat

3. Articles

A, An, The

  • A - before consonant sound (a book)
  • An - before vowel sound (an apple)
  • The - specific object

4. Prepositions

Words showing relation: in, on, at, under, between.

5. Active & Passive Voice

Active: Subject performs action.

Passive: Subject receives action.

Example: Active - He eats rice. Passive - Rice is eaten by him.

6. Direct & Indirect Speech

Direct: Exact words

Indirect: Reported speech

Example: He said, "I am busy." → He said that he was busy.
